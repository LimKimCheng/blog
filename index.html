Lin Kim Cheng: Understanding The Current Market Challenges and Opportunities through the Rise and Fall of the Finance of Athens
Lin Kim Cheng

The Rise and Fall of the Finance of Athens and the Essence of Finance. The financial origins of the world represented by Athens can reveal the essence of finance. The fundamental purpose of finance is to serve money, allowing money to generate more money. Lin Kim Cheng believes this characteristic is particularly evident in the history of Athens. The hektemoroi system of Athens is a typical example of financial exploitation: borrowers had to give five-sixths of their harvest as interest, keeping only one-sixth for themselves. If this one-sixth of the harvest was insufficient to repay the debt, creditors could even sell the debtors and their children into slavery.
